I can’t handle the passage of time and so I cling to the idea that just for me, it stands still.
That minutes and hours and days and years are fickle constructs, meant to satiate my body but not my mind. I pretend that time doesn't pass, because if it has not moved then I can pretend. Pretend that she is not gone and that they all still want to be my friend and that my childhood hasn’t slipped from my grasp and that I am okay with it all.
